About PayPilot AI

PayPilot is an agentic payment orchestration system that understands a user request, selects the right bill, checks risk and balance safety, chooses a payment method, asks for approval when needed, and records every decision for audit.

Run AI Tasks

The Orchestrator coordinates intent detection, planning, bill selection, risk scoring, method optimization, compliance checks, approval routing, execution, monitoring, and explanation.

Approval Routing

Low-risk payments can execute automatically. Medium-risk, duplicate, waste, high-value, or policy-sensitive payments pause and ask the user to open the Approval Queue before execution.

Explainable AI

The XAI panel and Agent Logs show why a bill was selected, what risk signals were found, which payment method was chosen, and why the final decision was allowed, blocked, or sent for approval.

Learning Signal

The Reinforcement Feedback Agent treats human approval and rejection outcomes as reward feedback. It adds a confidence signal to future orchestration without removing the human approval guardrail.

Account Rules

The Account page explains safe balance, approval threshold, autopay readiness, available payment methods, and risk watchlist items used by the compliance and optimizer agents.

Demo Flow

Start from Command Center, run an Orchestrator prompt, open the approval follow-up if required, approve or reject the payment, then verify the result in Transactions and Agent Logs.
